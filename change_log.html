ProteoSign Change Log

  • Added support for Replication Multiplexing! Now you can use ProteoSign for experiments where biological, technical replicates or conditions are represented either as different tags or as different MS runs. Visit our help page for more information!
  • We integrated expression enrichment analysis to ProteoSign's pipeline! Choose the target organism of your experiment in Step 3 and get useful information concerning your differentially expressed proteins' function - pathways etc.!
  • ProteoSign is now easy to install on a local server! Check our documentation for more information!
  • Venn diagrams are very useful to evaluate the reproducibility of your experiments! ProteoSign now generates Venn diagrams for proteins' quantification consistency across replicates and conditions. Our documentation will guide you in getting the most out of this new feature!
